Project Idea and Goals

Our group wants to build a Smart Waste Equity Dashboard focused on the city of Seattle. The core idea is to look at the relationship between where people are illegally dumping trash and where the city actually provides disposal resources. We want to see if "hotspots" of illegal dumping occur in areas that are "service deserts"; places far away from hazardous waste facilities. Additionally, we want to see how this dumping affects Seattle’s "Green Infrastructure" (like rain gardens and bioswales), which are easily damaged by pollutants. By the end of the quarter, we aim to have an interactive map that helps visualize whether the city’s waste resources are distributed equitably.
